Apply with your venture to the Startup Shell Founder's membership track. Join a fast-paced startup community of other founders with unique ventures. Get industry and alumi mentors, access to a collection of resources to grow your venture, and participate in an assortment of community events.

Fellow’s Track

Apply as an individual with a skillset in design, development, or business development to the Startup Shell Fellow's membership track. Build out your portfolio and get startup exposure by working with Startup Shell companies and other fellows. Also get access to resources, mentors, and community events.
