Join the Community

Membership is semesterly. Applications for Lambda batch open in August.
*Currently, only enrolled undergraduate or graduate students at the University of Maryland are eligible for membership.

Founders Track

Apply with your venture to the Startup Shell Founders membership track. Join a fast-paced startup community of other founders with unique ventures. Get industry and alumni mentors, access to a collection of resources to grow your venture, and participate in an assortment of community events.

Fellows Track

Apply as an individual with a skillset in design, development, or business development to the Startup Shell Fellows membership track. Build your portfolio and strengthen your skills by working with Startup Shell portfolio companies. Also get access to resources, mentors, and a community of entrepeneurs and innovators.
