Support Student-Initiated Courses
A Startup Shell component fund.

Startup Shell has partnered with Student-Initiated Courses (STICs) @ UMD. STICs is a program that helps students design, develop, and teach for-credit courses at the university. These courses exist in multiple departments such as music, computer science, math, art, business, and more.

STICs is proud to support student-facilitators by providing direct funding for their compensation. Your donation directly funds the stipend that these facilitators receive for designing, developing, and teaching for-credit courses.

  • AASP299Y: Introduction to Yoruba

  • MATH299C: Mathematics & Classical Music
