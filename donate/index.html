Donations Fuel Our Growth

Startup Shell is funded solely through donations. Your donation goes directly to providing the resources, space, and events needed for student-run companies to grow.

Donations have helped people like Ari grow the creative community in DC with her company BGOTI or helped Brett create the fastest consumer 3D printer on the market. These contributions create real impact for real students.

Startup Shell is a non-profit organization pursuant to Section 501(c)(3) of the United States Internal Revenue Code. Your donation to Startup Shell may qualify for an income tax deduction in accordance with Federal and/or State income tax laws. Please consult with your tax advisor to determine whether your donation is tax deductible in whole or in part. Nothing in the communication is intended to constitue legal or tax advice.
