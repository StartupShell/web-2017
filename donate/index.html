Donations Fuel Our Growth

Startup Shell is funded solely through donations. Your donation goes directly to providing the resources, space, and events needed for student-run companies to grow.

Donations help people like Nathalyn create new on-demand fashion solutions with her company POSH and Brett create the fastest consumer 3D printer on the market. These contributions create real impact for real students.

Don't have the capital to fund us directly? If you use Amazon at all, you can shop at smile.amazon.com instead of amazon.com and a portion of your purchase will go to Startup Shell! To get started, simply click the link above, search for and set your charity to Startup Shell Inc.
