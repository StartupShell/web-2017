Partner Contributions

Our partners are the organizations and people that keep us going; our community thanks you immensely for your support.

For large contributions, we have found that a direct bank transfer is the easiest method to process these contributions. The form on the right will allow you to securely complete a bank transfer through our donation, payments, and verification providers, Donorbox, Stripe, and Plaid respectively. Your bank account information is stored with Stripe for full PCI compliance.

Startup Shell is funded solely through donations. Your donation goes directly to providing the resources, space, and events needed for student-run companies to grow and expand.

Startup Shell is a non-profit organization pursuant to Section 501(c)(3) of the United States Internal Revenue Code. Your donation to Startup Shell may qualify for an income tax deduction in accordance with Federal and/or State income tax laws. Please consult with your tax advisor to determine whether your donation is tax deductible in whole or in part. Nothing in the communication is intended to constitute legal or tax advice.
